Glenview, Northbrook, and Deerfield consistently rank among Illinois' best places to live — a sweet spot of top schools, green space, and convenient access to both the city and the North Shore.

## Why people move here

Families relocate here for the schools and stay for the community. Modern downtowns, excellent parks, and a range of housing from townhomes to estate properties give buyers plenty of options.

## What Craig brings

Craig guides relocating families and longtime locals alike, drawing on decades of experience across the north and northwest suburbs.
