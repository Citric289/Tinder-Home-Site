Dunedin and Safety Harbor are two of the Gulf Coast's most charming towns — known for their walkable downtowns, waterfront parks, and laid-back coastal lifestyle just minutes from the beach.

## Why people move here

Buyers love the small-town character: local breweries, art festivals, the Pinellas Trail, and marina living. It's relaxed, friendly, and endlessly walkable.
