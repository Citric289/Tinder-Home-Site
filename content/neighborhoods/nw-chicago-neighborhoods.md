The Northwest Side neighborhoods — Edison Park, Norwood Park, and Jefferson Park — offer the best of both worlds: city living with a tight-knit, small-town feel.

## Why people move here

These communities are popular with first responders, families, and anyone who wants quick access to downtown without giving up a neighborly atmosphere. Bungalow-lined streets, local restaurants, and strong transit make them perennial favorites.
