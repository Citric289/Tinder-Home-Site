The Northwest Suburbs — Arlington Heights, Palatine, and Schaumburg — combine family-friendly neighborhoods with award-winning park districts and quick access to I-90 and the city.

## Why people move here

Buyers love the value: spacious homes, vibrant downtowns, and excellent schools at a more approachable price point than the North Shore. Metra lines and major employers nearby make commuting simple.
