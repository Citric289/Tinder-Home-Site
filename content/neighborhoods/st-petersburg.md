St. Petersburg has transformed into one of Florida's most exciting cities — a thriving arts scene, world-class waterfront dining, and a real estate market that's among the fastest-growing in the state.

## Why people move here

From the Dali Museum to a buzzing downtown, "St. Pete" blends culture with coastal living. Buyers find everything from historic bungalows to modern waterfront condos.
