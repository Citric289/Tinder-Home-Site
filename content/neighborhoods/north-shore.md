The North Shore is a string of prestigious lakefront communities — Evanston, Wilmette, Winnetka, and Highland Park — known for tree-lined streets, historic architecture, and some of the best schools in Illinois.

## Why people move here

Families are drawn to the top-rated public schools, walkable downtowns, and easy Metra access to the city. Lake Michigan beaches, parks, and a strong sense of community make it a place people stay for decades.
