Palm Harbor and Tarpon Springs offer top-rated schools, lush established neighborhoods, and a welcoming community — making them a favorite landing spot for families relocating from the Midwest.

## Why people move here

Families get the space and schools they want without sacrificing proximity to the beaches. Tarpon Springs adds historic Greek culture and a famous sponge-docks waterfront.

## What Craig brings

Relocation is Craig's specialty — he helps Chicago-area families settle into the Gulf Coast communities that fit their needs.
